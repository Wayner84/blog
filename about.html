About me

I’m Wayne. I build things: engineering prototypes, 3D prints, little software tools, and whatever else I get obsessed with for a week. This site is me being intentional about documenting it.

Why this exists

I’m good at doing the work — not great at showing it. So I’m treating this like a public lab notebook: short posts with photos, what I tried, what worked, what didn’t, and what I’d do differently next time.

What you’ll see here

  • 3D printed fixtures and prototypes (CAD → print → iterate)
  • Resin print experiments + support strategies
  • Small scripts and tools I build to speed up the work
  • Occasional “build logs” for furniture / shop improvements
